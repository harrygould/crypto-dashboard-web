Market Price

~ Why is Market Price useful? ~

Determines asset value, what buyers are willing to pay & sellers willing to accept

Allows for Technical Analysis Tools such as RSI & moving averages to identify entry & exit points

Helps identify general market sentiment due to price increases or decreases

Market Cap

(USD)

~ Why is Market Cap useful? ~

Measure the relative size of the asset in the context of its industry

Signals stability, with higher market cap deemed a more stable asset

Identify growth potential when used in conjunction with fundamental analysis

Trading Volume

(24 Hour)

~ Why is Trading Volume useful? ~

Sign of liquidity: how easily an asset can be bought or sold without changing its price

Signals market interest through participation

Helps identify a market trend or reversal pattern

~ Fun Bitcoin Facts! ~

Bitcoin is simply a C++ computer code protocol which is proof of a transaction between two entities

It was created by an 'anonymous' Software Engineer(s) in response to the 2008 banking collapse

There is a fixed supply of 21 million Bitcoin & the final Bitcoin will take about 40 years to mine

It offers financial privacy and freedom, as users can send money to anyone, anywhere, without relying on a central authority
